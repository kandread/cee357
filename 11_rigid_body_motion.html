Fluids in rigid-body motion

  • Container with fluid accelerates along a path or rotates about a fixes axis
  • Fluid will move as a rigid body
  • No relative motion \(\rightarrow\) no shear stress

Uniform linear acceleration

\[\nabla P = \rho (\overrightarrow{\mathbf{g}} - \overrightarrow{\mathbf{a}})\]

\[\theta = \tan^{-1}{\dfrac{a_x}{g + a_z}}\]

\[\dfrac{dP}{ds} = \rho \sqrt{a_x^2 + (g + a_z)^2}\]

Example

A drag racer rests a coffee mug on a horizontal tray while they accelerate at \(7\,\mathrm{m/s^2}\). The mug is 10-cm deep and 6-cm in diameter and contains coffee 7-cm deep at rest. Assuming rigid-body acceleration, will the coffee (\(\rho=1010\,\mathrm{kg/m^3}\)) spill out of the mug?

Will coffee spill?

\[\theta = \tan^{-1}{\dfrac{a_x}{g}} = \tan^{-1}{\dfrac{7.0}{9.81}} = 35.5^{\circ}\] If the mug is symmetric, then the rear side of the coffee will rise \[\Delta z = (3)(\tan{35.5^{\circ}}) = 2.14\,\mathrm{cm} < 3\,\mathrm{cm}\]

What is the gage pressure at point A?

At rest, \[P_A = \rho g h_{\text{rest}} = (1010)(9.81)(0.07) = 694\,\mathrm{Pa}\] When accelerating, \[P_A = \rho \sqrt{a_x^2 + g^2} \Delta s\] \[= (1010)\sqrt{9.81^2 + 7.0^2} \left[ (0.07 + 0.0214) \cos{35.5^{\circ}} \right] \approx 906\,\mathrm{Pa}\]

Rigid body rotation

Pressure distribution

\[\boxed{P = P_0 - \gamma z + \dfrac{1}{2} \rho r^2 \Omega^2}\] $$Ω: angular velocity \(P_0\): pressure at \((r,z)=(0,0)\)

Constant-pressure surfaces

\[z = \dfrac{P_0 - P_1}{\gamma} + \dfrac{r^2 \Omega^2}{2g} = a + br^2\]

Calculation of free surface

Center of the fluid drops by \(\dfrac{h}{2} = \dfrac{\Omega^2 R^2}{4g}\)

Example

Coffee cup is removed from the drag racer and put on a turntable that rotates about its central axis.

What is the angular velocity that will cause the coffee to reach the lip of the cup?

\[\dfrac{h}{2} = 0.03 = \dfrac{\Omega^2 R^2}{4g} = \dfrac{(9 \times 10^{-4}) \Omega^2}{(4)(9.81)}\] \[\Rightarrow \Omega^2 = 1308 \Rightarrow \Omega = 36.2\,\mathrm{rad/s}\]

What is the gage pressure at A?

\[P_0=0\] Point A at \((r,z)=(3,-4)\) \[P_A = P_0 - \gamma z + \dfrac{1}{2} \rho r^2 \Omega^2\] \[= 0 - (1010)(9.81)(-0.04) + \dfrac{1}{2}(1010)(0.03^2)(1308)\] \[=396 + 594 = 990\,\mathrm{Pa}\]
