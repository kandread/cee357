Fluid statics

The normal force exerted by a fluid per unit area is called…

Pressure and its SI unit is 1 Pa = 1 N/m2

The absolute, gage and vacuum pressures are related by…

\[P_{\text{gage}} = P_{\text{abs}} - P_{\text{atm}}\] \[P_{\text{vac}} = P_{\text{atm}} - P_{\text{abs}} = -P_{\text{gage}}\]

The variation of pressure with elevation/depth in a fluid at rest is given by…

\[\dfrac{dP}{dz} = -\rho g\]

The gage pressure in a static fluid open to the atmosphere at depth \(h\) from the free surface is given by…

\[P = \rho g h\]

The magnitude of the resultant force acting on a plane surface of a submerged plate is equal to…

\[F = (P_a + \rho g h_C)A\]

The center of pressure defines the…

line of action of the resultant pressure force

The vertical location of the line of action of the resultant force is given by…

\[y_P = \dfrac{I_{xx}\sin{\theta}}{h_C A}\] and it is relative to the centroid of the submerged surface

The upward force exerted on a submerged body by the fluid is called… and is given by…

The buoyant force is given by \[F_B = \rho_f g V\kern-0.8em\raise0.3ex-\]

A floating body is stable if the metacentric height is…

\[\overline{MG} = \dfrac{I_O}{V\kern-0.8em\raise0.3ex-_{\text{sub}}} - \overline{GB}\]

When a fluid that acts as a rigid body is linearly accelerated, pressure is expressed as…

\[P = P_0 - \rho a_x x - \rho (g + a_z) z\]

During rigid-body motion in a rotating cylinder, pressure is expressed as…

\[P = P_0 + \dfrac{\rho \Omega^2}{2} r^2 - \rho g z\]

Problem

A cylindrical sluice gate of unit length and radius 0.5 m, opens rotating about A when the level of the tank is \(h_w=3.5\,\mathrm{m}\). What is the magnitude and the line of action of the force exerted by the water for incipient opening condition? What is the weight of the sluice gate?

Solution

The horizontal component of the pressure force is given by \[ F_H = \gamma \left( h_w - \dfrac{R}{2} \right) R\] \[=(9790\mathrm{N/m^3})[(3.5 - 0.5/2)\mathrm{m}](0.5\mathrm{m})(1\mathrm{m}) \] \[ = 15908.75\mathrm{N} \]

\[ F_V = \gamma \left[ (h_w - R)R + \dfrac{\pi R^2}{4} \right]\] \[= (9790\mathrm{N/m^3})\left[ (3.5-0.5)(0.5) + \dfrac{\pi 0.5^2}{4} \right]\mathrm{m^2}(1\mathrm{m}) \] \[ F_V = 16607.26\mathrm{N} \] \[ \beta = \tan^{-1} \dfrac{F_V}{F_H} = \tan^{-1} \dfrac{16607.26}{15908.8} \] \[ = 46.23\mathrm{^{\circ}} \]

The moment of the water force at incipient opening conditions need to be equal to the weight moment \[ W R = |\mathbf{F}| R \sin \beta \] \[ \Rightarrow W = |\mathbf{F}| \sin \beta \] \[ W = F_V = 16607.26\mathrm{N} \]
