Specific energy

\[E = y + \dfrac{V^2}{2g}\]

For rectangular channels \[E = y + \dfrac{Q^2}{2gb^2y^2}\]

Specific energy and depth

  • Distance from point on the y-axis to curve is \(E\) at that \(y\)
  • Part between 1:1 line and curve is the kinetic energy head
  • \(y \rightarrow 0 \Rightarrow E \rightarrow \infty\)
  • Minimum value of \(E\) is the critical point
  • Flow can have two depths for a given value of \(E\) (alternate depths)

\[E = y + \dfrac{Q^2}{2gb^2y^2}\]

\[\dfrac{dE}{dy} = \dfrac{d}{dy} \left( y + \dfrac{Q^2}{2gb^2y^2} \right) = 1 - \dfrac{Q^2}{gb^2y^3} = 0\]

\[y_c = \left( \dfrac{Q^2}{gb^2} \right)^{1/3}\]

\[y_c^3 g b^2 = Q^2 = V_c^2 b^2 y_c^2 \Rightarrow V_c = \sqrt{gy_c}\]

Froude number

\[\text{Fr} = \dfrac{V}{\sqrt{gy}} = \dfrac{V_c}{\sqrt{gy_c}} = 1\]

  • At lower flow velocities and higher flow depths, flow is subcritical
  • At higher flow velocities and lower flow depths, flow is supercritical
  • Critical flow at minimum specific energy

Gradually varied flow

Form of steady nonuniform flow characterized by gradual variations in flow depth and velocity

\[H = z + y + \dfrac{V^2}{2g}\] \[\dfrac{dH}{dx} = \dfrac{dz}{dx} + \dfrac{dy}{dx} + \dfrac{V}{g} \dfrac{dV}{dx}\]

\[\dfrac{dH}{dx} = - \dfrac{dh_l}{dx} = -S_f\] \[\dfrac{dz}{dx} = -S_0\]

\[S_0 - S_f = \dfrac{dy}{dx} + \dfrac{V}{g}\dfrac{dV}{dx}\]

\[Q = ybV\]

\[\dfrac{dQ}{dx} = 0 = bV \dfrac{dy}{dx} + yb\dfrac{dV}{dx} \Rightarrow \dfrac{dV}{dx} = - \dfrac{V}{y} \dfrac{dy}{dx}\]

\[S_0 - S_f = \dfrac{dy}{dx} - \dfrac{V}{g}\dfrac{V}{y}\dfrac{dy}{dx}\]

\[S_0 - S_f = \dfrac{dy}{dx} - \dfrac{V^2}{gy}\dfrac{dy}{dx} = \dfrac{dy}{dx} - \text{Fr}^2 \dfrac{dy}{dx}\]

Gradually Varied Flow equation

\[\boxed{\dfrac{dy}{dx} = \dfrac{S_0 - S_f}{1 - \text{Fr}^2}}\]

Problem

  • 10-m wide rectangular horizontal channel
  • Water discharges from a sluice gate and undergoes hydraulic jump
  • Flow depth and velocity before the jump are 0.8 m and 7 m/s

\[y_1V_1 = y_2 V_2\]

\[P_1A_1 - P_2A_2 = \dot{m}_2V_2 - \dot{m}_1V_1\] \[\dfrac{\rho g y_1}{2} b y_1 - \dfrac{\rho g y_2}{2} b y_2 = \rho y_1 b V_1 (V_2 - V_1)\]

\[y_1^2 - y_2^2 = \dfrac{2y_1V_1}{g}(V_2 - V_1) = \dfrac{2y_1V_1^2}{gy_2} (y_1 - y_2)\]

\[\left( \dfrac{y_2}{y_1} \right)^2 + \dfrac{y_2}{y_1} - 2 \text{Fr}_1^2 = 0\]

\[\dfrac{y_2}{y_1} = 0.5 \left( -1 + \sqrt{1 + 8 \text{Fr}_1^2} \right)\]

What is the flow depth and the Froude number after the jump?

\[\text{Fr}=\dfrac{V_1}{\sqrt{gy_1}} = \dfrac{7}{\sqrt{(9.81)(0.8)}} = 2.5\]

\[\dfrac{y_2}{y_1} = 0.5 \left( -1 + \sqrt{1 + 8 \text{Fr}_1^2} \right)\]

\[\Rightarrow y_2 = 0.5(0.8)[-1 + \sqrt{1 + (8)(2.5)^2}] = 2.46\,\mathrm{m}\]

\[\text{Fr}_2 = \dfrac{V_2}{\sqrt{gy_2}} = \dfrac{(y_1/y_2)V_1}{\sqrt{gy_2}}\]

\[= \dfrac{(0.8/2.46)(7)}{\sqrt{(9.81)(2.46)}} = 0.464\]

What is the head loss and the energy dissipation ratio?

\[y_1 + \dfrac{V_1^2}{2g} = y_2 + \dfrac{V_2^2}{2g} + h_l\] \[\Rightarrow h_l = y_1 - y_2 + \dfrac{V_1^2 - V_2^2}{2g}\]

\[h_l = y_1 - y_2 + \frac{y_1 \text{Fr}_1^2}{2}\left( 1 - \dfrac{y_1^2}{y_2^2} \right)\]

\[h_l = (0.8) - (2.46) + \dfrac{(7)^2 - (7)^2(0.8/2.46)^2}{(2)(9.81)} = 0.572\,\mathrm{m}\]

Energy dissipation ratio \[\dfrac{h_l}{y_1 + V_1^2/2g} = \dfrac{h_l}{y_1(1 + \text{Fr}_1^2/2)}\]

\[\text{Dissipation Ratio} = \dfrac{0.572}{(0.8) + (7)^2/[(2)(9.81)]} = 0.173\]
