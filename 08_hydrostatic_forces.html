Reminder

\[F = \gamma h_{\text{CG}} A = P_{\text{CG}}A\] \[y_{\text{CP}} = - \dfrac{I_{xx} \sin{\theta}}{h_{\text{CG}} A}\] \[x_{\text{CP}} = - \dfrac{I_{xy} \sin{\theta}}{h_{\text{CG}} A}\]

Example

The gate is 5 ft wide, hinged at B. What is the force on the gate due to seawater pressure? What is the horizontal force exerted by the wall at A? What are the reactions at C?

What is the force on the gate?

Gate is 10 ft long from A to B, and CG is at 3 ft. \[h_{\text{CG}} = 15 - 3 = 12\,\mathrm{ft}\] \[F = P_{\text{CG}} A = \gamma h_{\text{CG}} A = (64)(12)(5)(10) = 384000\,\mathrm{lbf}\]

What is the horizontal force at A?

\[I_{xy} = 0\] \[I_{xx} = \dfrac{b L^3}{12} = \dfrac{(5)(10)}{12} = 417\,\mathrm{ft^4}\] \[l=-y_{\text{CP}} = \dfrac{I_{xx} \sin{\theta}}{h_{\text{CG}} A} = \dfrac{(417)(6/10)}{(12)(50)}=0.417\,\mathrm{ft}\] Distance from B to F is \(10-l-5=4.583\,\mathrm{ft}\) \[F_P L \sin{\theta} - F (5 - l) = 0 \Rightarrow F_P (6) - (38400)(4.583) = 0\] \[\Rightarrow F_P = 29300\,\mathrm{lbf}\]

What are the reactions at B?

\[\sum F_x = 0 = B_x + F \sin{\theta} - F_P\] \[\Rightarrow 0 = B_x + (38400)(0.6) - 29300\] \[\Rightarrow B_x = 6300\,\mathrm{lbf}\] \[\sum F_z = 0 = B_z - F \cos{\theta} = B_z - (38400)(0.8)\] \[\Rightarrow B_z = 30700\,\mathrm{lbf}\]

Problem

A tank of oil has a right-triangular panel near the bottom. Ignoring \(P_a\), find the hydrostatic force and the CP on the panel.

Solution

\[A=(1/2)(6)(12)=36\,\mathrm{m^2}\] \[I_{xx} = \dfrac{bL^3}{36} = \dfrac{(6)(12)}{36} = 288\,\mathrm{m^4}\] \[I_{xy} = \dfrac{b(b-2s)L^2}{72} = \dfrac{(6)[6-(2)(6)](12)^2}{72} = -72\,\mathrm{m^4}\] \[h_{\text{CG}} = 5 + 4 = 9\,\mathrm{m}\] \[F = \rho g h_{\text{CG}}A = (800)(9.81)(9)(36) = 2.54 \times 10^6\,\mathrm{N}\]

\[y_{\text{CP}} = - \dfrac{I_{xx} \sin{\theta}}{h_{\text{CG}}A} = -\dfrac{(288)\sin{30^{\circ}}}{(9)(36)} = -0.444\,\mathrm{m}\] \[x_{\text{CP}} = - \dfrac{I_{xy}\sin{\theta}}{h_{\text{CG}}A} = - \dfrac{(-72)\sin{30^{\circ}}}{(9)(36)} = 0.111\,\mathrm{m}\]
