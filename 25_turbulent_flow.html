Regions of turbulent flow near the wall

Viscous layer

  • Thickness very small (<1% of diameter)
  • Large velocity gradient
  • Essentially laminar flow

\[\tau_w = \mu \dfrac{u}{y} = \rho \nu \dfrac{u}{y} \Rightarrow \dfrac{\tau_w}{\rho} = \dfrac{\nu u}{y}\]

Friction velocity \[u_{\ast} = \sqrt{\dfrac{\tau_w}{\rho}}\]

\[\dfrac{u}{u^{\ast}} = \dfrac{y u^{\ast}}{\nu} \Leftrightarrow y^{+} = u^{+} \] Law of the wall

Overlap layer

\[\boxed{\dfrac{u}{u^{\ast}} = \dfrac{1}{\kappa} \ln{\dfrac{y u^{\ast}}{\nu}} + B}\]

Logarithmic law

\(B \approx 5.0\) and \(\kappa \approx 0.41\)

Outer turbulent layer

\[\dfrac{u}{u^{\ast}} = 2.44 \ln{\dfrac{y u^{\ast}}{\nu}} + 5.0\]

\(u_{\text{max}}\) at pipe centerline \[\dfrac{u_{\text{max}}}{u^{\ast}} = 2.44 \ln{\dfrac{R u^{\ast}}{\nu}} + B\]

\[\dfrac{u_{\text{max}} - u}{u^{\ast}} = 2.44 \ln{ \dfrac{R}{R- r}}\] Deviation of velocity from centerline velocity defect

Velocity profile in core region of flow is independent of viscosity!

Quiz

Average velocity

\[\dfrac{u(r)}{u^{\ast}} \approx 2.44 \ln{\dfrac{(R - r)u^{\ast}}{\nu}} + 5.0\]

\[V = \dfrac{1}{\pi R^2} \int_0^R u^{\ast} \left[ 2.44 \ln{\dfrac{(R-r)u^{\ast}}{\nu}} + 5.0 \right] 2 \pi r dr\]

\[\dfrac{V}{u^{\ast}} \approx 2.44 \ln{\dfrac{R u^{\ast}}{\nu}} + 1.34\]

\[\dfrac{V}{u^{\ast}} = \left( \dfrac{\rho V^2}{\tau_w} \right)^{1/2} = \left( \dfrac{8}{f} \right)^{1/2}\]

\[\dfrac{R u^{\ast}}{\nu} = \dfrac{(1/2)V D}{\nu} \dfrac{u^{\ast}}{V} = \dfrac{1}{2} \text{Re} \left( \dfrac{f}{8} \right)^{1/2}\]

Moody chart

Types of fluid flow problems

  1. Given D, L, and V or Q compute head loss or pressure drop
  2. Given D, L, and head loss compute velocity or flow rate
  3. Given Q, L, and head loss compute pipe diameter
  4. Given Q, D, and head loss compute pipe length

Problem

  • Oil (ρ=950 kg/m3 and ν=2 × 10-5 m2/s)
  • Flows through 30-cm pipe diameter, 100-m long
  • Head loss of 8-m
  • Roughness ratio ε/D=0.0002
  • Find average velocity and flow rate

Solution

\[f = h_l \dfrac{D}{L} \dfrac{2g}{V^2} = (8)\dfrac{0.3}{100} \dfrac{2(9.81)}{V^2}\] \[f V^2 \approx 0.471\] \[f=0.014 \Rightarrow V=\sqrt{0.471/0.014}=5.8\,\mathrm{m/s} \Rightarrow \text{Re} \approx 87000\] \[\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{f}} = -2.0 \log{ \left( \dfrac{\epsilon / D}{3.7} + \dfrac{2.51}{\text{Re} \sqrt{f}} \right)}\]

\[f=0.020 \Rightarrow V=\sqrt{0.471/0.020}=4.85\,\mathrm{m/s} \Rightarrow \text{Re} \approx 72800\] \[\boxed{f=0.0201}\] \[V = 4.85\,\mathrm{m/s}\] \[Q = V \dfrac{\pi D^2}{4} = 0.343\,\mathrm{m^3/s}\]
