Work

  • Energy interaction is work if associated with a force
  • Time rate of doing work is power \(\dot{W}\)
  • Numerous forms of work

\[W_{\text{total}} = W_{\text{shaft}} + W_{\text{pressure}} + W_{\text{viscous}} = W_s + W_p + W_{v}\]

Shaft work

  • Pumps, turbines, fans, compressors
  • Shaft protrudes through the control surface

\[\dot{W}_s = \omega T_s = 2 \pi \dot{n} T_s\]

Pressure work

\[\delta W = P A \delta s\]

\[\delta \dot{W}_p = P A V_{\text{piston}}\]

\[\delta \dot{W}_p = - P dA V_n = - P dA \left( \vec{V} \cdot \vec{n} \right)\]

\[\dot{W}_p = - \int_A P \left( \vec{V} \cdot \vec{n} \right) dA\]

Shear work

Work due to viscous stresses at the control surface \[\dot{W}_v = - \int_{\text{CS}} \vec{\tau} \cdot \vec{V} dA\]

  • Solid surface: no-slip condition \(V=0\)
  • Machine surface: Included in shaft work
  • Inlet or Outlet: Normal stress very small

Applying the Reynolds Transport Theorem

\[\dfrac{dB_{\text{sys}}}{dt} = \dfrac{d}{dt} \int_{\text{CV}} \rho b d V\kern-0.8em\raise0.3ex- + \int_{\text{CS}} \rho b \left( \vec{V_r} \cdot \vec{n} \right) dA\]

\[B=E~~~~~b=e\]

\[\dfrac{\left( dE \right)_{\text{sys}}}{dt} = \dfrac{d}{dt} \int_{\text{CV}} e \rho d V\kern-0.8em\raise0.3ex- + \int_{\text{CS}} e \rho \left( \vec{V}_r \cdot \vec{n} \right) dA\]

\[\dot{Q} + \dot{W}_s + \dot{W}_p + \dot{W}_v = \dfrac{d}{dt} \int_{\text{CV}} e \rho d V\kern-0.8em\raise0.3ex- + \int_{\text{CS}} e \rho \left( \vec{V}_r \cdot \vec{n} \right) dA\]

\[\dot{W}_p = - \int_{\text{CS}} \dfrac{P}{\rho} \rho \left( \vec{V}_r \cdot \vec{n} \right) dA ~~~~~~~ \dot{W}_v=0\]

\[\boxed{\dot{Q} + \dot{W}_s = \dfrac{d}{dt} \int_{\text{CV}} e \rho d V\kern-0.8em\raise0.3ex- + \int_{\text{CS}} \left( \dfrac{P}{\rho} + e \right) \rho \left( \vec{V}_r \cdot \vec{n} \right) dA}\]

Problem

  • Power generated is measured to be 1862 kW and the generator efficiency is 95%
  • What is the overall efficiency of the turbineâ€“generator?

Solution

Assume irreversible losses in the pipes are negligible.

\[\Delta e_{\text{mech}} = \cancelto{0}{\dfrac{P_1 - P_2}{\rho}} + \cancelto{0}{\dfrac{V_1^2 - V_2^2}{2}} + g (z_1 - z_2)\]

\[=gh = (9.81\,\mathrm{m/s^2})(50\,\mathrm{m})=490.5\,\mathrm{J/kg}\]

\[\Delta E = \dot{m} \Delta e = (5000\,\mathrm{kg/s})(0.491\,\mathrm{kJ/kg}) = 2455\,\mathrm{kW}\]

\[\eta = \dfrac{\dot{W}_{\text{out}}}{\Delta E} = \dfrac{1862\,\mathrm{kW}}{2455\,\mathrm{kW}}\] \[= 0.76\] or 76%
